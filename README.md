# Click-Wheel-Revenue & Pofitability -Analysis
This project shows visibility into revenue and profitability performance for the Retail management to better understand which product categories drive revenue and profit from 2016-2020 of a fictional dataset.
 
# Data
•	Global Retail sales dataset which includes sales data, product data, and customer data.

# Tools
•	Power BI

•	Excel

# What I Did
•	Cleaned and prepared the global retail sales data.

•	Created measures for total revenue, total profit, total cost, profit margin, and total orders
•	Built an interactive Power BI dashboard with KPIs, trends, and category analysis
•	Analysed profitability and performance differences across product categories

Key Highlights
•	YTD Revenue: $54.72M
•	YTD Profit : $32.05M
•	Profit Margin: 59% 
•	Top Product Category: Computers
•	Top Performing Continent and States: North Americas and the United States

General Insight
The overall profit margin is 59%, meaning the business retains $0.59 in profit for every $1 of revenue, indicating strong cost efficiency and healthy profitability though profitability varies by product category.

Key Insights
•	Computers generate the highest profit, contributing over $11M, making it the most valuable category
•	Games and Toys contribute the lowest profit, indicating limited margin impact ie. Not all high-revenue products are equally profitable
•	Overall profit margin remains stable at approximately 59%, despite revenue fluctuations
•	Revenue and profit show clear seasonal trends toward month-end.
